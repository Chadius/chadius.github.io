Monthly Highlight: The Zoommaster 500!

This month's highlight is the Zoommaster 500. As you can see, its an extremely flexible vehicle that can fulfill many purposes.

Ever wanted to fly to the cape, then drive up and down the coast?. The Zoommaster 500 fits into a briefcase, so you can carry it on an airplane at a moment's notice (make sure to tell the agent about it though- it's sure to set off the metal detectors.)

It's quite fuel efficient, too. A pint of gas will get you across the country. Of course, there is a solar-powered battery that will go for a long time before.

I'd give this 5 stars. Highly recommended...if you can afford it.
