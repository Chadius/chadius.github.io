Monthly Highlight: The Zoommaster 500!

(Hover over the picture for a special surprise!)

This month's highlight is the Zoommaster 500. As you can see, its an extremely flexible vehicle that can fulfill many purposes:

Features

  • Fuel efficient: A pint of gas will get you across the country. Of course, there is a solar-powered battery that will go for a long time before.
  • Low-altitude flight: Ever wanted to fly to the cape, then drive up and down the coast? The Zoommaster 500 can maintain its elevation for short distances.
  • Compact: The Zoommaster 500 fits into a briefcase, so you can carry it on an airplane at a moment's notice (make sure to tell the agent about it though- it's sure to set off the metal detectors.)

Rating

I'd give this 5 stars. Highly recommended...if you can afford it.
