This month's highlights!

Here are a few of my favorite vehicles.

Mechafast-Z

The Mechafast-Z is built for raw speed. Too bad its suspension means you'll be bouncing back and forth with every bump on the road.

Rating

4 stars.

MotorLine

The MotorLine is a curious vehicle. Price is paramount and it performs well for its cost.

Rating

3 stars.

Zoommaster 5000

This month's car of the month is the Zoommaster 500. As you can see, its an extremely flexible vehicle that can fulfill many purposes.

Rating

5 stars.
